Sleep

Improve Your Sleep

Sleep is extremely vital for the human body to function well. Here is how much sleep you need based on your age:

Consequences of too little sleep

  • Chronic health problems
  • Depression and anxiety
  • Decreased brain function
  • Memory loss
  • Weakened immune system

Consequences of too much sleep

  • Headaches
  • Heart disease
  • Back pain
  • Obesity
  • Greater risk of dying

Common causes of bad sleep

  • Noise
  • Room temperature too hot or cold
  • Uncomfortable beds
  • Looking at screens before bed
  • Alcohol, caffeine or nicotine
  • Eating too much in the late evening

*Therefore, try to create a good sleep schedule and prepare a good sleeping environment before bedtime to attain the rest that your body needs : )*
