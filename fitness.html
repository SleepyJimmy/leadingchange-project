How to incorporate Fitness into your lifestyle?

  • Increase the amount of physical activity you do in a day
  • Walk or cycle to destinations if possible, instead of driving or taking the bus
  • Implement a workout routine to do before or after work
  • Always increase the intensity of workouts to progress forward
  • Join a physical sport that you are interested in

Picking up a sport is a fun way to stay active!
