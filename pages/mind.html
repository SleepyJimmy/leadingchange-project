Mind

Improve your Mental Wellbeing

Having good nutrition and sleep while staying physically active are all factors to good mental health.

However, there are many other factors that can affect your mental state.

Other factors that can improve your mental health

  • Reframe unhelpful thoughts
  • Be in the present moment (mindfulness)
  • Connect with other people!
  • Do things that you enjoy
  • Learn new skills

Other factors that can impair your mental health

  • Leading an easy life without effort
  • Not enough physical exercise
  • Lack of Sleep
  • Inadequate diet
  • Having a victim mentality

“Go ahead and live with your head held high! No matter how devastated you may be by your own weakness or uselessness…set your heart ablaze. Grit your teeth and look straight ahead.”
